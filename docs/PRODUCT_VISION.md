# Product Vision

## One-liner

Bardo converts phones on the same WiFi into a synchronized collaborative speaker.

## Problem

Groups often want music in informal situations but do not have a speaker. One phone is not loud enough, especially when people are talking or there is ambient noise.

## Target users

Initial users:

- Young groups with low budget.
- Friends at a small party or pre-party.
- Students.
- People hanging out outdoors.
- Any group with several phones but no speaker.

## Initial promise

> No tenés parlante. Tenés Bardo.

## Product principle

The group already owns enough hardware. It is just distributed across everyone’s pockets.

## Long-term vision

Bardo should learn how sound behaves in a room or outdoor space, compensate for device latency, reduce perceived echo, and keep phones synchronized even when people move.
